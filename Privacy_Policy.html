MT Prime Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02 April 2026

1. Introduction

MT Prime respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the MT Prime platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information – such as your name, email address, and login details.
  • Usage Information – such as films watched, ratings, views, and interactions on the platform.
  • Filmmaker Information – such as uploaded content details, producer name, and payout-related information.
  • Technical Information – such as device type, browser, IP address, and connection details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide video streaming and platform features
  • Track views, ratings, and platform analytics
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Review filmmaker uploads and manage content distribution
  • Communicate important updates, support messages, or notices

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

MT Prime may use cookies or similar technologies to keep users signed in, remember preferences, and improve platform functionality.

You may control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect some platform features.

5. Content, Views, and Analytics

MT Prime may collect information about views, watch activity, ratings, and general platform engagement for analytics, performance measurement, and filmmaker reporting.

  • View counts may be used to calculate content popularity and revenue sharing.
  • Ratings may be stored to improve recommendations and content feedback.
  • Analytics may be used to improve platform quality and business decisions.

6. Sharing of Information

MT Prime does not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers that help operate the platform
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect MT Prime, its users, filmmakers, or partners from fraud or abuse
  • With filmmakers in limited form, such as aggregated analytics or content performance data

7. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

However, no online service is completely secure, and MT Prime cannot guarantee absolute security of all data.

8. User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account or data
  • Object to certain uses of your information

9. Retention of Data

MT Prime keeps your information only as long as necessary to operate the platform, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

10. Children’s Privacy

MT Prime is not intended for children unless specifically permitted under applicable law and with appropriate parental or guardian involvement where required.

11. Third-Party Services

MT Prime may use third-party tools and services such as hosting, analytics, authentication, cloud storage, or payment services. These services may collect or process data according to their own privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

MT Prime may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Continued use of the platform after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, support, or data concerns, contact us at:
