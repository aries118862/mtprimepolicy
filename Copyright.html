MT Prime Copyright & DMCA Policy

Effective Date: 02 April 2026

1. Introduction

MT Prime respects intellectual property rights and expects all users and filmmakers to do the same. This policy outlines how copyright complaints and infringement claims are handled on the platform.

2. Ownership of Content

Filmmakers and content creators retain full ownership of their content uploaded to MT Prime. By uploading content, creators confirm that:

  • They own the content or have legal rights to distribute it
  • The content does not infringe any copyright, trademark, or intellectual property rights

3. Copyright Infringement

It is strictly prohibited to upload, share, or distribute content that violates copyright laws or the rights of others.

  • Unauthorized use of films, music, or media is not allowed
  • Repeated violations may result in account termination

4. Filing a DMCA Complaint

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on MT Prime without permission, you may submit a copyright complaint including:

  • Your full name and contact details
  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • Link or location of the infringing content
  • A statement that you believe the use is unauthorized
  • A statement that the information is accurate and you are the rights owner

5. Action on Complaints

Upon receiving a valid complaint, MT Prime may:

  • Remove or disable access to the infringing content
  • Notify the uploader of the complaint
  • Suspend or terminate accounts for repeated violations

6. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you may submit a counter-notice including your contact details and a statement explaining your rights to the content.

MT Prime may restore content if the claim is successfully disputed.

7. Repeat Infringers

Accounts that repeatedly violate copyright rules may be permanently banned from MT Prime.

8. Contact for Copyright Issues

To report copyright infringement or submit a DMCA request, contact:

support.mtprime@gmail.com
